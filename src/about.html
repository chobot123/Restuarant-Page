About our Founder
Ever since his return from Willy Wonka's Chocolate Factory, Augustus Gloop quickly tired of the monotone flavors found in the World's candy. After all, he had tasted it all at his short stay in the legendary chocolate factory. To satiate his unending appetite, he turned to another alternative - meat. Like a child mesmerized by a new toy, he sought out for all kinds - and the best - of meat; and as they say, beef is king. So when the best of Wagyu failed to impress him, he in turn decided to embark on a new adventure, to create and serve the best. So here we are, and we welcome you here, to Beef!
Our Mission Statement
Our goal here is simple: to have you indulge in the finest of beef. Embrace your carnivorous side.
